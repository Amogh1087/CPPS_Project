How do I analyze my resume? ▼
Go to the Analyzer page, upload your PDF or DOCX, type a job role, and click Analyze Now.
Where are my reports saved? ▼
Reports are saved in your browser's local storage — only on this device and browser.
Is my data stored online? ▼
No. Resume text is used only for the AI analysis and never stored on any server.
How do I export a report? ▼
Click "Print / Export PDF" inside any report to open your browser's print dialog. Save as PDF from there.
What does ATS mean? ▼
Applicant Tracking System — software used by employers to filter resumes before a human reads them.
