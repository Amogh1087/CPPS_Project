How do I analyze my resume?▼
Upload a PDF or DOCX in the Analyzer page, type your target job role, and click Analyze Now. Results appear within 30 seconds.
What file formats are supported?▼
PDF and DOCX files up to 5 MB. Ensure your PDF has selectable text (not a scanned image).
How is the score calculated?▼
The AI evaluates Content Quality, Skills Match for your target role, Structure & Format, and ATS Compatibility. Score is a weighted average of these four.
Is my resume stored?▼
No. Resume text is used only for the AI analysis and never stored on any server. Reports live in your browser's local storage only.
What is ATS?▼
Applicant Tracking System — software employers use to filter resumes before a human reads them. High ATS score = better keyword and format alignment.
How do I improve my score?▼
Follow the Suggestions and add Missing Skills from your report. Quantify achievements, use clear section headers, and match keywords to the job role.
