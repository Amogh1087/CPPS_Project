How do I analyze my resume? ▼
Upload a PDF or DOCX file in the Analyzer page, type your target job role, then click "Analyze Now". Results appear within 30 seconds.
What file formats are supported? ▼
PDF and DOCX files up to 5 MB. Make sure your PDF has selectable text (not a scanned image).
How is the score calculated? ▼
The AI evaluates Content Quality, Skills Match for your target role, Structure & Format, and ATS Compatibility. The overall score is a weighted average of these four dimensions.
Is my resume stored on a server? ▼
No. Your resume text is sent to the AI for analysis and never stored. Reports are saved only in your browser's local storage.
Where are my saved reports? ▼
All reports are stored in your browser's local storage. They are only accessible on this device and browser. Use "My Reports" to view them.
What does ATS mean? ▼
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software used by employers to filter resumes before a human reads them. A high ATS score means your resume uses keywords and formatting that pass these filters.
How do I improve my score? ▼
Follow the Suggestions and add the Missing Skills listed in your report. Quantify your achievements (e.g. "increased sales by 30%"), use clear section headers, and tailor keywords to the specific job role.
