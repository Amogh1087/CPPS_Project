How do I analyze my resume? ▼
Upload a PDF or DOCX, type your target job role, then click "Analyze Now". Results appear within 30 seconds.
What file formats work? ▼
PDF and DOCX up to 5 MB. Ensure your PDF has selectable text (not a scanned image).
How is my score calculated? ▼
The AI evaluates Content Quality, Skills Match, Structure & Format, and ATS Compatibility — scored 0–100 each.
Is my data stored? ▼
No. Resume text is only used for analysis and never stored on any server. Reports live in your browser's local storage only.
What is ATS? ▼
Applicant Tracking System — software employers use to filter resumes before a human reads them. High ATS score = better keyword and format alignment.
How to improve my score? ▼
Follow the Suggestions list and add Missing Skills. Quantify achievements, use clear section headers, and match keywords to the job role.
